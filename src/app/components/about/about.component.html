About me

I'm a web developer, based in Brooklyn, NY.

I currently work as a naval architect and marine engineer in New York City, but am transitioning into the field of web development.

In September 2017, I began a full-stack intensive course at CUNY City Tech, where I began my my studies in web developement. HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, PHP, MySQL, and some other web tech goodies (jQuery, Bootstrap4, git).

During that course, I hit the ground running, creating numerous projects that tested my new found knowledge:

  • I developed an application for roommates who wish to keep track of chores, household purchases, and potential guests that may come to visit. (PHP, MySQL, jQuery, AJAX, Bootstrap4). See Roomies
  • I created a mockup of a non-profit site that included Bootstrap features (modals, carousels, accordians). See NYJTL
  • A collection of small vanilla javascript apps that implement new ES6 features. See Bayground

In March, I decided to keep up with the more emerging web technologies and took an intermediate front-end course at NYU.

Here, I was introduced to NodeJS, NPM, Webpack, Express, MongoDB, Angular and React.
